How Does It Work?

Teodor Malchev, lead developer of the matching process, what are the basics behind HY's algorithm?

All you have to do is register, fill out the matching form, and let us do the rest! Here is how our magical algorithm works:

  1. Categorization of Preferences: We start by categorizing the information you provide in our form into two types: obligatory and optional. The obligatory factors are non-negotiable preferences that form the foundation of our matching process.

  2. Formation of Unique Groups:Based on these obligatory preferences, we divide students into 15 unique groups. This ensures that essential requirements are met in every match.

  3. Categorization of Preferences: We start by categorizing the information you provide in our form into two types: obligatory and optional. The obligatory factors are non-negotiable preferences that form the foundation of our matching process.

  4. Incorporation of Optional Preferences:Next, we use your optional preferences to fine-tune the matching process. This step focuses on aligning the expectations and desires for the stay, helping us pair guests and hosts from the same obligatory groups who are seeking a similar experience.

  5. Dynamic Matching Process:If initial matches are not found, our algorithm dynamically shifts unmatched users to adjacent preference groups. This iterative process continues, increasing the likelihood of finding a compatible match, while still prioritizing the core obligatory preferences.

  6. Balancing Demand and Supply:We recognize that there are often more guests seeking housing than available hosts. In such cases, our algorithm works tirelessly to maximize matches. However, it's important to note that finding a match for every user can be challenging when facing a surplus of guests.

  7. Objective and Unbiased:Our algorithm is designed to be 100% objective and unbiased. It operates solely on the preferences you provide, ensuring fairness and impartiality in every match.

  8. Final Outcomes:While our system strives to match every user, in instances where an imbalance between guests and hosts exists, some guests may remain unmatched. We sincerely apologize if you find yourself in this situation. Please understand that this is a reflection of the numbers, not the effectiveness of our algorithm or your personal preferences.

  9. Commitment to Perfect Matches:Our goal is to ensure that every match is as perfect as possible, aligning guests with hosts who share similar preferences and expectations.
